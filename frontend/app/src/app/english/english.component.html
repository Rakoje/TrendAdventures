Canyoning

Canyoning is an exciting and adventurous activity that involves exploring canyons by hiking, rappelling, swimming, and sometimes even jumping. It's a thrilling experience that can take you to some of the most stunning natural wonders in the world, but it's not without risks.

That's why it's important to always have experienced guides on your canyoning trips. Canyoning guides are trained professionals who have extensive knowledge of the canyons, the local environment, and the necessary safety techniques. They know how to navigate the terrain, identify potential hazards, and respond to emergencies should they arise.

Having an experienced guide by your side can make all the difference in ensuring that your canyoning adventure is both safe and enjoyable. They can provide you with the necessary equipment, teach you proper techniques for rappelling and swimming, and guide you through the canyon's twists and turns.

Welcome to Nevidio Canyon, a hidden gem in Montenegro that's waiting to be explored! Tucked away in the heart of the Durmitor National Park, this rugged canyon is a must-visit destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike.

Nevidio Canyon is one of the last remaining canyons in Europe that's largely unexplored and untouched by humans. It's a breathtaking natural wonder that features steep cliffs, cascading waterfalls, crystal-clear pools, and narrow gorges that are just waiting to be traversed. One of the best ways to experience Nevidio Canyon is by going on a canyoning tour. This thrilling activity involves hiking, swimming, abseiling, and rock climbing through the canyon, guided by experienced professionals who will ensure your safety at all times.

On your canyoning tour, you'll get to explore the hidden corners of Nevidio Canyon, discovering its many natural wonders along the way. You'll be able to swim in emerald pools, slide down natural water slides, jump off cliffs into deep pools of water, and abseil down sheer drops – all while surrounded by the stunning beauty of the canyon.

No previous experience is required to go on a canyoning tour in Nevidio Canyon, but you should be in good physical shape and be comfortable in the water. The tours are suitable for anyone aged 8 and above, and all necessary equipment is provided.

If you're looking for an unforgettable adventure in Montenegro, be sure to add a canyoning tour in Nevidio Canyon to your itinerary. It's an experience you won't forget!

Krapina is located just a short drive from Budva, but it feels like a world away from the hustle and bustle of the city. The village is surrounded by rolling hills, olive groves, and vineyards, and it's the perfect place to unwind and escape the crowds.

One of the must-visit attractions in Krapina is the Drenostica canyon, popular destination for canyoning enthusiasts, offering an exciting opportunity to explore the rugged terrain of Montenegro. Your canyoning tour will take you through a maze of narrow passages, steep cliffs, and rocky terrain, all while surrounded by breathtaking natural scenery.

As you make your way through Drenostica Canyon, you'll have the chance to rappel down waterfalls, swim in refreshing pools, and jump into deep water. This thrilling adventure is a must-do for anyone seeking an adrenaline rush and an unforgettable experience in Montenegro. In addition to canyoning, Drenostica Canyon is also a popular destination for hikers and nature lovers. The surrounding hills offer stunning views of the canyon and the surrounding countryside, making it an ideal place to explore on foot.

After your canyoning adventure, you can relax and unwind in the charming village of Krapina, where you'll find traditional Montenegrin hospitality and delicious local cuisine. Be sure to try some of the local specialties, such as freshly-caught fish, grilled meat, and homemade rakija (a traditional fruit brandy).

Whether you're a thrill-seeker or a nature lover, Drenostica Canyon and the village of Krapina have something for everyone. Come and explore this hidden gem in Montenegro and create memories that will last a lifetime!
